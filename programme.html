Our Educational Programmes

At Jets Elementary, we offer a holistic curriculum designed to nurture curious minds, build strong character, and prepare children for the future. Discover the pillars of our educational approach.

CBC-Aligned Learning

Our curriculum is fully aligned with the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC). We move beyond rote memorization to focus on developing practical skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities. Learning is personalized, allowing each child to demonstrate understanding in ways that suit their strengths, ensuring they are not just educated but truly equipped for real-world challenges.

Modern ICT Exposure

In today's digital world, technological literacy is fundamental. Our students gain hands-on experience in our dedicated computer lab, learning basic digital skills, safe internet practices, and introductory coding concepts. This early exposure demystifies technology, fostering digital confidence and creativity from a young age.

Safe and Caring Environment

We believe a child learns best when they feel secure and valued. Our school prioritizes emotional and physical safety above all. Our trained staff fosters a warm, inclusive, and supportive atmosphere where every child is known, heard, and encouraged. This nurturing foundation is essential for academic risk-taking and personal growth.

Balanced Co-curricular Activities

Education extends beyond the classroom walls. A well-rounded schedule of sports, music, drama, art, and clubs ensures the development of the whole child. These activities teach teamwork, discipline, resilience, and leadership, providing vital outlets for energy and passion while helping children discover and hone their unique talents.

Play-Based Early Years Program

For our youngest learners, play is the primary vehicle for discovery. Our early years program is designed around structured and free play that naturally builds literacy, numeracy, social, and motor skills. Through stories, songs, manipulatives, and imaginative play, we ignite a lifelong love for learning in a joyful and pressure-free setting.

Strong Moral and Social Values

Academic excellence is paired with character development. We actively teach and model core values such as integrity, respect, responsibility, empathy, and kindness. Through daily interactions, community service projects, and value-based lessons, we guide children to become not only smart students but also compassionate and ethical citizens.
