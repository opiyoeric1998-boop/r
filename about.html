About Jets Elementary School

At Jets Elementary School, we provide a nurturing environment where young minds are empowered to reach their full potential. Our commitment to excellence in education, character development, and holistic growth makes us a leading choice for parents in Kisumu and beyond.

Our Story

Jets Elementary School was founded in August 2023 by its visionary founder, Madam Diannah Omariba, with a clear mission: to provide a nurturing, learning centered environment where every child has the opportunity to grow, discover,and succeed.Jets opened its door with an initial enrollment of 50 learners.

Despite being a young institution, the school quickly demonstrated its commitment to excellence.Within just two months of operation, jets proudly celebrated its first graduating learner, making a significance milestone in its early journey. today jets elementary stands as a growing international minded institution, committed to excellence, inclusivity, and preparing young learners to thrive in global community.

What makes Jets Elementary unique is our holistic approach to education. We believe that true learning extends beyond textbooks and classrooms. Our curriculum integrates academics with life skills, creativity, and social responsibility, preparing our students not just for exams, but for life itself.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our educational approach

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, student-centered education that empowers children to become confident, compassionate, and responsible global citizens. We strive to create a learning environment that celebrate diversity,encourages critical thinking, and fosters a lifelong love of learning.

Our Vision

We envision a school where every child discover their unique talents, develops strong character, and acquires the skils needed to thrive in an ever changing world. Our vision is to be a leading elementary school known for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and a strong sense of community.
