Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our school

What makes jets elementary stand out?

We blend strong academics, modern learning resources, passionate teachers, and a warm, family friendly environment that helps every learner thrive.

Do you communicate with parents regularly?

Absolutely! We keep parents updated through meetings, messages, and weekly reports so you always know your child's progress.

What co-curricular activities do you offer?

From music, art, sports, cooking, debate, science fair, environmental club, young entrepreneurs, fashion and modelling, Tae Kwon Do to help your child discover new passions beyond the classroom.

How can I enroll my child?

It's easy! Visit the school, call our office, or start enrollment online. Our team will guide you step by step.

What curriculum do you teach?

We proudly teach the CBC curriculum, using fun, hands-on learning that helps every child discover their talents and grow confidently.

What ages do you admit?

We welcome young learners from playgroup to Grade 4, giving them a strong, happy start in school.

How do you keep children safe?

Safety is our top priority. We have secure grounds, clean classrooms, trained teachers, and strict child protection procedures.
