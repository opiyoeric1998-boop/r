Our Approach to Science Education

At Jets Elementary, our Science and Technology program is designed to transform students into curious investigators and creative problem-solvers. We believe science is not just a subject to be memorized, but a way of thinking—a process of asking questions, making observations, and discovering how the world works.

Our curriculum, fully aligned with the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), empowers young minds to think like scientists and engineers from an early age, moving beyond rote learning to genuine understanding.

The Core of Inquiry

Applying the Scientific Method in real experiments

Our methodology is rooted in Inquiry-Based Learning. Students don't just receive facts; they actively construct understanding through the Scientific Method—learning to ask questions, form a hypothesis, conduct observation and experimentation, and draw evidence-based conclusions.

We integrate principles of design thinking, encouraging students to identify problems, brainstorm solutions, build prototypes, and iterate on their designs. This process is the foundation for developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for the 21st century.

Learning Domains (Key Topics)

Our program explores the wonder of science across three interconnected domains

Biological Sciences

Investigating living things, life cycles, ecosystems, and human biology through observation and exploration of our natural world.

  • Plant & Animal Life
  • Human Body Systems
  • Ecosystems & Environment

Physical Sciences

Uncovering the principles of matter, forces, energy, sound, and light through experiments and hands-on discovery.

Exploring weather, geology, the solar system, and environmental stewardship through observation and investigation.

Hands-on Exploration & Our Lab

True scientific understanding comes from doing. Our program is built around hands-on experiments and project-based learning. To support this, we are proud of our Dedicated Science Lab, a safe and well-equipped space where curiosity comes to life.

Here, students move beyond textbooks to test theories, use scientific tools, measure, record data, and experience the thrill of discovery firsthand. This practical environment is where abstract concepts become tangible understanding through real-world problem solving.

Technology Integration

Digital tools enhance scientific discovery

Technology at Jets Elementary is a powerful tool for scientific exploration and expression. We integrate computational skills and digital literacy seamlessly into our science program.

Students use tablets and computers for virtual simulations, interactive models, and research. They learn to code simple programs to control models or represent data, use digital tools for data logging and analysis, and create multimedia presentations to communicate their findings.

This fusion ensures our students are not just consumers of technology, but adept users and creators who can leverage digital tools to enhance their scientific understanding.
